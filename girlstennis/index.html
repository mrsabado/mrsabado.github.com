  • Monday: October 22, 2012

    Individual League Finals: Click the following links for the Singles Draw, Doubles Draw, and Doubles Feed-In Draw.

    All matches will play an 8-game Pro-set. If you lose in the "Feed-In Draw," you will be automatically eliminated.

    If you lose in the Main-Draw, you will play in a Consolation Round until you lose again. Matches in the Consolation Round will be a regular 6-game set with ads.

    Semi-Final Matches will be a best of 3 set match.

    Finals Matches will be an 8-game Pro-Set.
