  • Monday: August 27, 2012

    Boys Tennis Tryouts will be held in the month of January. All participants of the tryouts will be selected by me and Assistant Coach Robinson dependent on different criteria like: skill, teamwork, attitude, etc.

    Varsity will be comprised of approximately 10-13 boys and Junior Varsity will also have 10-13 boys. There is no age requirement for the Varsity Team. Any athlete on varsity must demonstrate that he can positively contribute to the team.
